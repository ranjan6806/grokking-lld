
Played on 64 squares in 8*8 grid

Each player has
    16 pieces
    1 king
    1 queen
    2 rooks
    2 knights
    2 bishops
    8 pawns

Moves
    rook - horizontally or vertically
    knight - L shape
    bishop - diagonally
    queen - in any direction
    king - one square in any direction
    pawns - forward one square but capture diagonally
